About Us

Welcome to New Legacy Barbershop, a unique and personal barbering experience that began in 2023. Our barbershop is independently owned and operated, reflecting a true passion for the craft of barbering.

We believe in delivering quality service wherever we are. Our founder, a self-employed barber, runs the business entirely on his own. While we don't yet have a permanent salon space, our services are available from Bizana Nomlacu during holiday seasons and Durban CBD after the holidays.

Using a mobile approach, we ensure loyal clients receive the best care, wherever the location. At New Legacy Barbershop, it's all about creating a lasting legacy of excellence.
