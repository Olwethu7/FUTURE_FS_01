About Us

New Legacy Barbershop began in 2023 and is independently owned, reflecting a true passion for barbering.

We offer mobile services in Bizana Nomlacu during holidays and Durban CBD year-round, ensuring loyal clients get the best care wherever they are.

Our goal is to create a lasting legacy of excellence in barbering.
